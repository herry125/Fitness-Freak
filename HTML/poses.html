POSES BASED ON TYPE , BENEFIT , GENERAL AND ANATOMY


POSES BASED ON TYPE


1.Low-impact aerobics:

This type of exercise is ideal for beginners or those with joint pain. It involves movements that keep one foot on the ground , such as walking, swimming, or cycling.

2.High-impact aerobics:

This type of exercise is more intense and involves movements that require both feet to leave the ground, such as jumping jacks, burpees, or running.

3.Dance aerobics:

This type of exercise combines dance moves with aerobic exercise. Examples include Zumba, Jazzercise, or hip-hop dance classes.

4.Step aerobics:

This type of exercise involves using a raised platform or step to perform movements such as stepping up and down, lunging, or squatting


POSES BASED ON BENEFITS


1.Sun Salutation:

This is a sequence of 12 yoga poses that can be performed in a flow. It is great for warming up the body and increasing blood circulation.

2.Warrior II:

This pose strengthens the legs, opens the hips, and improves balance. It also helps to increase stamina and endurance .

3.Chair Pose:

This pose strengthens the legs, glutes, and core muscles. It also helps to improve posture and balance .

4..Boat Pose:

This pose strengthens the core muscles and improves digestion. It also helps to reduce stress and anxiety .


POSES BASED ON GENERAL


1.Side Leg Lifts:

Stand with feet together and lift one leg out to the side, then lower it and repeat on the other side.

2.Front Leg Kicks:

Stand with feet together and kick one leg forward, then lower it and repeat with the other leg.

3.Cross-Country Skiing::

Mimic the motion of cross-country skiing by moving your arms and legs alternately.

4.Fast-Paced Dancing:

Dance to your favorite music with fast, rhythmic movements.


POSES BASED ON ANATOMY


1.Neck Tilt:

Gently tilt your head from side to side to stretch the neck muscles.

2.Shoulder Rolls:

Roll your shoulders forward and backward to release tension in the shoulders.

3.Child's Pose:

Kneel on the floor and sit back on your heels, reaching your arms forward for a gentle spinal stretch.

4..Cat-Cow Stretch:

Get on your hands and knees, arch your back like a cat, then lower your belly and lift your head for a cow stretch.
