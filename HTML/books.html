REFERENCE BOOKS BASED ON AEROBIC EXERCISES


Aerobic exercise is a physical activity that uses your body's large muscle groups, is rhythmic and repetitive. It increases your heart rate and how much oxygen your body uses. Examples of aerobic exercises include walking, cycling and swimming. It reduces your risk of heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure and high cholesterol.

Here we are recommending you some books for your better way of learning and practicing.These manuals offer useful exercise regimens for creating, enhancing, and sustaining skill. Every day of the week has practice material available in the books. If you adhere to these routines, your playing will benefit from increased speed, dexterity, accuracy, coordination, and more.

The book provides step-by-step instructions, illustrations, and practical advice on how to perform strength training exercises safely and effectively. It also includes information on the benefits of strength training, the importance of proper nutrition, and strategies for staying motivated
